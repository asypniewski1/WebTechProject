ABOUT ME

welcome to cooking diary by ak

My name is Anna Katherine Sypniewski and I am a senior at Babson College. I love to cook for my family and friends, so I decided my project will be a webpage dedicated to the recipes I make! Some recipes I find online, some are from cookbooks, and some are family recipes!

My webpage includes appetizer, dinner, dessert, and baked good recipes. Each page will include 5 recipes that I have loved!
