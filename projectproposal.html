My Project Proposal - My Cooking Website

  • What is your website about?
    1. As I have mentioned on previous assignments, I love to cook. I always take pictures of the foods I cook and provide links to where I get recipes. My website will be about the foods I've cooked and provide pictures, a description about the food, and a link to the online recipe.
  • What is the objective of your website?
    1. The objective of the website will be to share fun recipes with the public in hopes of inspiring them to cook themselves.
  • Who is your target audience?
    1. My target audience will be those who already cook and need inspiration as well as those who want to get into cooking. The range of the recipes I cook go from easy-hard, so there is a recipe for every level of cook.
  • What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?
    1. I am going to have seperate pages for appetizers, main course, dessert, and pastries.
